Playing with Some GSAP Animations

May 23, 2017

Over the weekend I was looking at some GSAP Animation tutorials. GSAP was not as hard to get started with as I thought it would be. Although I am not very proficient, yet, in JavaScript, understanding some of the basics of GSAP was not that hard. I decided to play around with animating some boxes and some dots. I made three different animations. The first animation is with five boxes. I had the boxes slide in from the right. The second animation is with circles. I animated their background color and border-radius. The last animation was with dots that formed my name. They change color as well as their size. I'm still tweaking the third animation. I will put it on code pen when I am finished. Here is the link to the first two animations on code pen. I really enjoyed using GSAP and look forward to playing with it again.
