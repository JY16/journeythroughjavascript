About Me

Hello, I'm Jamie. I am a Front End Developer. Though I know the basics of JavaScript I am still learning the more intermediate/advanced aspects of JavaScript. Along with JavaScript, I am learning Sass, Git/GitHub, and jQuery. After I have a solid understanding of these, I will go on to learn node and react and all that they require. To further my skills, I will learn some other languages/technologies for both my professional and personal development.

How did I get into web development? Well, it was not intentional. It started back in 2012. I was looking for electives for Fall semester. I came across an Intro to Internet/Web class. I had never give any thought to how websites were made. I was interested so I enrolled. In the Summer of 2013, I found myself going through the textbook again and learning more HTML and CSS. In 2014, I decided to change my major from Business Administration to Web Technologies and took a few more classes. When it came time for me to learn JavaScript, I felt that I was not ready. (I was wary of JavaScript. I did not want to believe or accept that I had to learn JavaScript.) Since 2015, I have been teaching myself web development with various online resources. When I was "ready" to learn JavaScript it did not go well. It was hard for me to find really good learning resources for JavaScript and I had gotten a little discouraged. It was not until recently that I found quality learning resources and began to really understand it.

I am looking forward to learning all that I develop a desire and/or need to learn and to the apps, software, and websites, maybe even a game, that I will make.
